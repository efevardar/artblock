From the creator

Hi! Thanks for checking Art Block.

I'm Efe, the creator of Art Block and an artist working in the video game industry for over 8 years.

The idea for Art Block came from a workout tracker I've been using for the past year. I'd never been able to stay consistent at the gym before, but that app made consistency feel surprisingly easy.

It drove home how much I thrive on solid scheduling: showing up with goals already clear for the session, and the motivation of watching stats stack up over time. I wanted that same scaffolding for my art practice, not only for the gym.

Art school had given me part of that rhythm years ago; since then, my own practice had been missing that structure. Art Block began as a tool for myself. Since then it has helped me stay honest and on track, and I'm putting it out there in case it does something similar for you.

Feel free to reach out with any suggestions on how I can improve the app!

—Efe
